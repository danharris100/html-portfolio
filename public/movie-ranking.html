The Best Films According To Daniel

My top 5 films of all time

The Life of Brian

Should I like it? Is it appropriate? I'm not sure but what I do know is that it's flipping funny. Some of the best film quotes of all time. Try not to feel guilty and just enjoy it.

Anchorman

A comedy classic, laugh out loud funny. Some of the best comedy characters ever created with a ton more quotes to remember and use.

Paddington 2

I probably shouldn't like this as much as I do but I can't help it. Without doubt Hugh Grant's best acting (although a fairly low bar). It makes prison look quite appealing.
